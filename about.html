A Little Bit About Me

Hey there, lovely! I'm Roberta — a lifestyle blogger, devoted Christian, unapologetic The Office fan (I'm not superstitious, but I am a little stitious), fitness enthusiast, and entrepreneur based in the beautiful city of Accra, Ghana.

I created A Little Bit Stitious as a space to share the things that bring me joy: from my favorite beauty products and skincare routines to faith-filled devotionals, book recommendations, travel adventures across Ghana and beyond, and tips for living a more intentional life.

When I'm not blogging, you can find me at the gym, journaling with a cup of tea, trying out new recipes, or quoting Michael Scott at completely inappropriate times. I believe in living life with grace, gratitude, and a whole lot of laughter.

I'm so glad you're here. Pull up a chair, grab your favorite drink, and let's be friends!
